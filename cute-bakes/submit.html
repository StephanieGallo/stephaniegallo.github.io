Submit your recipes & photos

Do you have a recipe that you would love to share? Or maybe a photo of the cutest cake you've ever baked? Users are welcome to submit their photos, recipes, or comments & questions! Below are community guidelines for recipe submission. If you still have questions, contact us at support@cutebakes.com

Recipe Submission Guidelines

  • The suggested length for recipes is 500-750 words or less.
  • Recipes must include a brief introduction.
  • Ingredients and preparation must be clearly listed in bullets.
  • If possible, include nutritional/caloric information as well as dietary specifications (ex: Vegan, nut free, soy free, etc.)
  • Link to specific products mentioned in your recipe and list measurements clearly.
  • Recipes must include photos of the actual finished product (not a stock photo). Please submit professional and/or high quality food photos. No phone cameras, bad lighting, etc will be accepted. Photo format: web-optimized PNG or JPEG.
  • If you have been compensated by any brand that is referenced in your recipe, you need to clearly disclose this information to us.
  • Recipes will be reviewed and may be edited to comply with our guidelines prior or after publication.

We reserve the right to reject recipes.

Terms of Submission

By sending this submission to Cute Bakes, you are representing that you are the author and copyright owner of the submission and/or have all rights necessary to provide this submission (including images) to Cute Bakes.

What Next?

If your submission is chosen to be featured, you will be notified via email with a link to the web page your content is posted on. If you no longer wish you have your content on our website, contact us at support@cutebakes.com
