Making baking fun and easy

Welcome to Cute Bakes. Cute Bakes was created to show that making cute baked goods can be fun and easy! You can find easy recipes, decorating techniques, photos of baking inspirations, and more. You can explore different recipes and techniques from beginner to expert.

I’ve been baking for over 10 years and have baked countless  cookies, cakes, cupcakes, pies, and cake pops. I’ve successfully baked for parties and various events. I hope you enjoy your time and find the information on this website helpful, inspirational, and enjoyable. 4th of July Sugar Cookies

Total Time: 2 hr 24 min
Prep: 15 min
Cook: 9 min
Yield:about 3 dozen-2 1/2 inch
Difficulty:Easy

Ingredients

3 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup unsalted butter, softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg, beaten
1 tablespoon milk
Powdered sugar, for rolling out dough
